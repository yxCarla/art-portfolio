Welcome To Carla's Art Portfolio

(website built entirely from scratch)

In this site I will have my artwork displayed dating from 2015 to present (2022). Most of these are traditional graphite on paper, with some digital inclusions. Watermarks or signatures are present on all traditional work.

Materials

  • Photos of artwork were taken with an Apple iPhone 11
  • 0.5 mm (Pentel), 0.3 mm (Muji) mechanical pencil
  • graphite pencils (ProArt & Prismacolor 4H to 8B)
  • blending stumps (#5 and #3), tortillons (#3 and #2)
  • kneaded eraser, regular eraser (Hipolymer)
