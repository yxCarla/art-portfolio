Welcome To Carla's Art Portfolio

In this site I will have my artwork displayed dating from 2016 to present (2022). Most of these are traditional graphite on paper, with some digital inclusions. Watermarks are present on all traditional work.

Materials used include
  • 0.5 mm (Pentel), 0.3 mm (Muji) mechanical pencil
  • graphite pencils (ProArt & Prismacolor 2H to 8B)
  • blending stumps (#5 and #3), tortillons (#3 and #2)
  • kneaded eraser, regular eraser (Hipolymer)
