(website built by me)

In this site I will have my artwork displayed dating from 2017 to present (2022). Most of these are traditional graphite on paper, with some digital inclusions. Watermarks are present on all traditional work.

Photos of artwork were taken with an Apple iPhone 11

Materials used include
  • 0.5 mm (Pentel), 0.3 mm (Muji) mechanical pencil
  • graphite pencils (ProArt & Prismacolor 2H to 8B)
  • blending stumps (#5 and #3), tortillons (#3 and #2)
  • kneaded eraser, regular eraser (Hipolymer)
